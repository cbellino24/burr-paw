Guides

Use and Care

Wear it. Sweep it. Clean it. Everything you need to get the most out of your Burr Paw — from first pass to cleanup.

  • Wear Like a mitt, or fold for a quick job
  • Sweep Burrs stick to the mitt, not your hands
  • Clean Rub together — or scrape with the card

How it works

Flexible mitt. Simple sweep.

The Burr Paw is worn on the hand and conforms to irregular surfaces — shoelaces, fleece, even your pet’s ear. With a sweeping motion, stickers and burrs adhere to the mitt instead of your fingers.

  • Worn on the hand like a mitt
  • Folded in its stowed position for a quick job
  • Folded if you suspect a burr may penetrate the liner

Field guide

Three moves. Done.

Same rhythm every time — whether you’re cleaning a dog, a jacket, or a pack.

  1. 01

    Wear it

    Slip it on like a mitt so the paw can wrap irregular shapes. For a fast spot job — or when you want extra liner protection — fold it into the stowed position and work that way.

  2. 02

    Sweep it

    Use a simple sweeping motion across clothing, gear, or pets. Stickers and burrs grab onto the Burr Paw surface so you spend less time picking them off by hand.

  3. 03

    Clean it

    When the mitt fills up, rub it together like you’re working out a stain — the stickers and burrs fall away. Need more help? Pull the cleaning card from the pocket under the Burr Paw logo, hold the mitt by its tag on a flat surface, and scrape.

Why it matters

Built for the mess you actually have.

Faster cleanup, salvageable gear, and a safer alternative to picking burrs with bare fingers.

  • Saves time over other removal methods
  • Salvages clothing and gear ruined by stickers and burrs
  • Relieves the discomfort of nuisance stickers and burrs
  • Helps prevent the spread of invasive plants
  • Puncture-resistant liner is a safer alternative to removing burrs with your fingers*

*Warning: Liner is puncture resistant, not puncture proof. Extra care should be taken with various types of stickers and burrs.
