Use and Care

Everything you need to get the most out of your Burr Paw — from first sweep to cleanup.

Benefits

  • Saves valuable time over other methods of removal
  • Salvages clothing and gear deemed unusable due to stickers and burrs
  • Provides relief from the discomfort of nuisance stickers and burrs
  • Environmentally friendly — helps prevent the spread of invasive plants
  • Puncture-resistant liner is a safer alternative to removing burrs with your fingers*

*Warning: Liner is puncture resistant, not puncture proof. Extra care should be taken with various types of stickers/burrs.

How it works

The Burr Paw is flexible and worn on the hand. While using it, the paw easily conforms to irregularly shaped surfaces — like shoelaces or your pet’s ear. With a simple sweeping motion, stickers and burrs adhere to the surface of Burr Paw.

  • Worn on the hand like a mitt
  • Folded in its stowed position for a quick job, or if you suspect a burr may penetrate the liner

Cleanup

When the Burr Paw becomes full of stickers and burrs, simply rub the mitt together as if rubbing out a stain. The stickers and burrs will fall out. If needed, remove the cleaning card from the pocket under the Burr Paw logo, hold the mitt by its tag on a flat surface, and scrape.
