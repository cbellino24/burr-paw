Use and Care

Wear it. Sweep it. Clean it. Everything you need to get the most out of your Burr Paw — from first pass to cleanup.

  • Wear Like a mitt, or fold for a quick job
  • Sweep Burrs stick to the mitt, not your hands
  • Clean Rub together — or scrape with the card

Field guide

Three moves. Done.

Same rhythm every time, whether you’re cleaning a dog, a jacket, or a pack.

Ready to try it?

Watch the walkthrough, then grab a colorway that fits your kit.

Watch demo Shop Burr Paw
  1. 1

    Wear it

    Slip it on like a mitt so the paw can wrap irregular shapes. For a fast spot job, or when you want extra liner protection, fold it into the stowed position and work that way.

  2. 2

    Sweep it

    Use a simple sweeping motion across clothing, gear, or pets. Stickers and burrs grab onto the Burr Paw surface so you spend less time picking them off by hand.

  3. 3

    Clean it

    When the mitt fills up, rub it together like you’re working out a stain. The stickers and burrs fall away. Need more help? Pull the cleaning card from the pocket under the Burr Paw logo, hold the mitt by its tag on a flat surface, and scrape.
